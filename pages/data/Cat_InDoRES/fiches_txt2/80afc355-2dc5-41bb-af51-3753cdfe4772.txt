Data were check for outliers in unvariate trait distribution across and within species, as well as on bivariate relationships well described previously in the literature. Consistencies across years of measurements were also checked. 
Abundance data were measured by Maud Bernad-Verdier in the extensive management regime (Bernard-Verdier, M., Navas, M.-L., Vellend, M., Violle, C., Fayolle, A., & Garnier, E. (2012).

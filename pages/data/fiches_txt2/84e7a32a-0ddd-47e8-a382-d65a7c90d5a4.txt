SOERE aims at understanding and modelling the dynamics and ecological functioning of lake ecosystems affected by local and global forcing factors.
Three peri -alpine lakes (Lakes Geneva , Annecy, Bourget) and their watersheds are monitored. These systems represent important natural resources and meet key societal demands (drinking water, fishing, tourism, biodiversity ... ) .

The services provided by the Soere relates to both long-term in situ observation and experimentation.

The observatory task is based on logistic, technical and analytical supports for (i) collecting environmental data (physico- chemical and biological), (ii) creating and managing database (ii) organizing the storage of reference biological samples. The accession to the data is possible according to the 'Terms & Conditions of Use' of the Soere Database.

The SOERE OLA can provide logistic supports for sampling, in situ measurements and experimentation on Lake Geneva. This service is submitted to validation according to the " Logistic Support Leman" service charter.

1. FLORISTIC DATA
Visual estimates of vascular plants abundance (based on Braun-Blanquet scale) in plots of subalpine and alpine vegetation
This is part of a long-term monitoring program for evaluating environmental changes in alpine landscapes
The main considerations in designing the approach were comparability, simplicity and economy to cover a large number of high elevation ecosystems

One record every hour or every two hours
From October 2007 onwards for most sites
Longer time series (back to 1999) available for a few sites

One record every hour

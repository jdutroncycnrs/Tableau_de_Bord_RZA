It has been designed to allow hazard measurements to be carried out in the field, using standardized protocols.
CoastAppli is built in an interdisciplinary way, both from a scientific and pedagogical point of view. Facilitating the information of indicators through an application allows the adhesion of more managers, and the integration of coastal users in the data collection (as it is the case for applications like Rivages, or Coastsnap).

Objectives: 
Facilitate reliable, accurate, and timely measurements in the field. 
Involve all the public in the monitoring of coastal hazards on a territory.
Acquire scientific data (harvestable by each user, experiment or not) and use them to better understand the evolution of erosion and submersion hazards, in order to anticipate related problems.

Uses:
There are different profiles depending on the experience of the user (beginner, experienced, manager).
The protocols are standardized and adaptable for all types of coastlines (beaches, dunes, cliffs, artificial coasts).
The data collection process is rigorous and usable for scientific studies.

Functionalities:
The application is available for download above and for all Android smartphones. It is free so that everyone can participate in the monitoring of coastal hazards!
Simple measurement tools are integrated into the application for both novice and experienced users. Professional measurement tools can be used with the application's protocols by managers.
Information on the study sites is available in order to know their specificities (description, history, hazards...).
